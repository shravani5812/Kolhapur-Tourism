Kolhapur Thali

Kolhapur is renowned for its rich and spicy cuisine, which is a significant part of its cultural heritage. The food here is characterized by the use of local spices, especially the famous Kolhapuri masala, which gives dishes their unique flavor.

Popular Dishes

Experience the fiery taste of Misal, Tambada Rassa, Pandhara Rassa and Kolhapuri Thali, famous for their bold spices and unique flavors. Kolhapuri cuisine is a blend of Maharashtrian and Konkani influences, known for its rich use of spices like red chili, garlic, and coconut.
