Exploring kolhapur

Kolhapur has a vibrant online presence with several blogs covering diverse topics such as travel, food, real estate, and local culture. These blogs offer insights into the city's attractions, cuisine, and way of life. IndiBlogger and The Times of India are good starting points for discovering Kolhapur-related blogs. Here's a more detailed look at what you can find: Travel & Tourism: Places to visit: Blogs highlight popular attractions like the Mahalaxmi Temple, New Palace, Panhala Fort, and various markets. Travel experiences: Bloggers share their personal travelogues, providing tips and recommendations for exploring Kolhapur. Local events: You can find information on local festivals, cultural events, and other happenings in the city. Food & Culture: Kolhapuri cuisine: Many blogs focus on the famous Kolhapuri cuisine, particularly its non-vegetarian dishes like Tambada Rassa and Pandhara Rassa, as well as local snacks and sweets. Cultural aspects: Blogs delve into the unique culture of Kolhapur, including its traditional jewelry (like Kolhapuri Saaj), local markets, and the "Pheta" (turban). Food reviews and recommendations: You can find reviews of restaurants and cafes, along with recommendations for must-try dishes and experiences.
